## Competitive Landscape

### Samsung
Samsung is Apple's closest rival in smartphones, wearables, and technological innovation. They compete directly across multiple consumer electronics categories, particularly with their Galaxy line of smartphones and smartwatches.

### Alphabet
Alphabet, through its Google subsidiary, competes with Apple via its Android mobile operating system, Google Play Store, and its own Pixel smartphones. It rivals Apple in mobile software, hardware, and app ecosystem.

### Huawei
Huawei competes with Apple primarily in the smartphone market, maintaining a strong influence, especially in China, with its HarmonyOS and a wide range of devices despite international sanctions.

### Xiaomi
Xiaomi rapidly expands across emerging markets, dominating the mid-range smartphone segment. They compete with Apple by offering feature-rich smartphones at more accessible price points.

### Amazon
Amazon competes with Apple in tablets with its Kindle devices against the iPad, and in AI services (Alexa vs. Siri). Additionally, its cloud platform, Amazon Web Services, rivals Apple's own cloud infrastructure and services.
